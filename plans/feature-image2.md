# Tana Keyboard — Google Play Feature Image Prompt

## Context

Tana Keyboard (also called Addiyon Keyboard) is an Android keyboard app that lets users type Amharic (Ethiopic/Ge'ez script) by typing Latin letters — "selam" instantly becomes `ሰላም`. It also has a full English keyboard. The app targets Ethiopian diaspora and Amharic learners worldwide.

## Composition (1024 x 500)

### Center

A realistic Android phone mockup (slight 3D tilt, ~10° left, with soft drop shadow). The phone screen shows the keyboard in action — light theme, QWERTY layout with white keys on a light gray tray (`#ECEEF0` tray, `#FFFFFF` keys). The space bar reads `አማርኛ`. Small Ge'ez characters appear as corner previews on each letter key. A suggestion strip above the keyboard shows Amharic word completions in fidel script. The composing buffer at the top of the keyboard shows Latin text being typed (e.g., "sel") with the Amharic transliteration `ሰል` appearing as the primary suggestion.

### Left Side

The app icon — a vermillion red (`#EE4D2D`) squircle (rounded square) with a white Ge'ez character `አ` (glottal stop "a") with two horizontal vermillion bars crossing through it (stencil-cut effect). Place it floating with a subtle shadow, slightly above center-left.

### Right Side

Bold headline text in Poppins Bold font, warm ink color (`#17150F`):

- **Line 1:** "Type Latin. Get Fidel."
- **Line 2** (smaller, Poppins Medium, stone gray `#8A8578`): "The Amharic keyboard for Android"

### Bottom Strip

A thin accent bar in vermillion (`#EE4D2D`) spanning the full width, with small white text: "Addiyon Keyboard" in Poppins SemiBold.

### Background

Soft warm paper tone (`#F4F1EA`) with a very subtle vertical gradient and faint decorative sine-wave curves in vermillion at ~6% opacity (similar to the app's PaperBackground effect). Clean and minimal, not distracting.

## Visual Style

Modern, clean, Material 3 aesthetic. Flat design with subtle depth (soft shadows, no harsh edges). Warm, inviting color palette. The overall feel should communicate: "This is a polished, professional keyboard app that makes Amharic typing effortless."

## Mood

Helpful, modern, culturally proud but approachable. Not cluttered — let the fidel script characters be the visual star.
